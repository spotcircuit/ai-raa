AI Revenue Acceleration Audit

A Self-Assessment for B2B Tech CROs

Goal: Assess how ready your revenue engine (sales + marketing) is to use AI to shorten sales cycles and close more deals. Score each question (1-5) using the key below, then tally your total. Focus: Fast, proven AI wins.

Scoring Key:
1 = Not Started: No capability—losing deals and time.
2 = Basic: Minimal efforts, inconsistent revenue impact.
3 = Developing: Process exists, but not optimized for speed or wins.
4 = Strong: Solid execution, driving some revenue lift.
5 = Fully Optimized: Best-in-class, maximizing deal velocity and close rates.

1. Data for Deals

Getting the right data in place is the foundation for targeting high-value accounts quickly. AI speeds up data cleanup and enrichment, reducing qualification time to close deals faster.

Question 1: Is your CRM data clean and centralized enough to pinpoint high-potential accounts fast?

Example: 1 = Siloed, outdated; 3 = Centralized but messy; 5 = Unified, actionable daily.

Why It Matters: Clean data cuts lead qualification time by 30% (SiriusDecisions).

Question 2: Can you enrich account data (e.g., firmographics, revenue) without breaking the bank?

Example: 1 = No enrichment; 3 = Manual updates; 5 = Auto-enriched via tools like ZoomInfo.

Why It Matters: Enriched data boosts targeting precision—15% more conversions (Forrester).

Example: 1 = No LLM use; 3 = Basic manual research; 5 = LLMs pull web insights for personas.
